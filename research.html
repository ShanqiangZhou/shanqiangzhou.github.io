RESEARCH & INSIGHTS

Market Overview – A World Defined by Liquidity and Regime Shifts

Financial markets today are shaped less by short-term headlines and more by long-term regime transitions: aging demographics, shifting monetary structures, global supply reconfiguration, and the rise of intangible capital. At Wentworth Holdings, our research focuses on understanding these structural drivers rather than predicting short-term volatility.

Japan: A Market Rediscovering Itself

Japan remains one of the most compelling investment landscapes globally. Corporate governance reforms, rising wages, improving domestic demand, and a breakaway from decades of deflation are setting the foundation for a prolonged equity re-rating.

The Rise of Global Consumer Franchises

Sanrio exemplifies a cultural franchise with multigenerational emotional value. Brands with global recognition and recurring relevance tend to outperform market cycles—especially when guided by strong capital discipline and international expansion.

Innovation & Asymmetric Assets

Certain sectors, particularly those at the frontier of digital transformation, offer asymmetric return profiles. Metaplanet represents a category where market narratives lag behind fundamental optionality. Our research emphasizes identifying nonlinear upside rather than forecasting short-term movements.

Investment Framework – How We Form Conviction

1. Fundamentals Over Forecasts
We analyze unit economics, capital allocation, management integrity, and durability of demand.

2. Behavior Over Noise
Markets misprice assets due to emotional overreaction. We focus on value hiding in periods of uncertainty.

3. Time Arbitrage
Most participants operate on short cycles. We operate on a multi-year horizon where true compounders shine.

Looking Ahead

We continue to see strong opportunities in Japanese consumer brands, innovation-led companies, and businesses with pricing power and improving capital efficiency.
