RISK MANAGEMENT

A Discipline of Protection and Long-Term Stewardship

At Wentworth Holdings, risk management is not a separate function—it is the foundation of every investment decision we make. Our philosophy centers on protecting capital first, compounding capital second.

Our Risk Philosophy

We believe risk is defined not by volatility but by permanent loss of capital, weak fundamentals, poor governance, excessive leverage, and uncertainty in long-term demand.

Key Sources of Risk We Evaluate

  • Business Fundamentals – competitive advantage, return on capital, resilience.
  • Financial Structure – leverage, liquidity, reinvestment capacity.
  • Management Integrity – capital allocation and decision quality.
  • Market & Macro Conditions – interest rates, inflation regimes, global liquidity.
  • Behavioral Risks – sentiment extremes and narrative-driven mispricing.

Our Risk Management Framework

  • Fundamental Research – long-term intrinsic value assessment.
  • Scenario Analysis – evaluating outcomes across economic conditions.
  • Position Sizing Discipline – no single holding threatens the portfolio.
  • Continuous Monitoring – reviewing earnings, competition, and governance.
  • Time Horizon Advantage – focusing where long-term value emerges.

Capital Protection – Our Highest Priority

Every investment must satisfy three conditions: survival under stress, intrinsic value growth, and a sufficient margin of safety. If any condition is uncertain, we do not invest.

Looking Forward

As global markets evolve, we remain committed to protecting capital, avoiding permanent losses, and building resilient portfolios guided by clarity, discipline, and long-term thinking.
