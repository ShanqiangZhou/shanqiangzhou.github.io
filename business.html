BUSINESS DIVISIONS

FOCUSED MANDATES. ONE PHILOSOPHY.

Wentworth Holdings is organized into focused investment divisions, each built around a disciplined process and a clearly defined mandate. Together, these divisions allow us to allocate capital efficiently across opportunities while maintaining a consistent philosophy and risk framework.

Public Equity Strategies

Our Public Equity Strategies division focuses on listed companies with attractive risk–reward profiles. We seek high-quality businesses trading at a discount to intrinsic value, with an emphasis on cash-flow durability, balance-sheet strength, and management alignment. Portfolios are concentrated and benchmark-agnostic, reflecting our highest-conviction ideas.

Long-Term Holdings

The Long-Term Holdings division is dedicated to compounders—businesses we believe can grow intrinsic value steadily over many years. We are prepared to hold through cycles, provided fundamentals remain intact and capital allocation remains rational. This division reflects our belief that time in outstanding businesses is more powerful than timing the market.

Tactical Opportunities

Our Tactical Opportunities division targets mispriced or event-driven situations, including dislocations, structural changes, and special situations where the market has overreacted or misinterpreted risk. While position sizes are controlled, these opportunities can enhance overall portfolio returns when pursued with discipline and downside protection.

Research & Strategy

Underpinning every division is our Research & Strategy group, which provides deep fundamental analysis, macro context, and scenario work. This team ensures that insights are shared across strategies, that risks are viewed holistically, and that capital is deployed where the probability-adjusted return is most compelling.
